At the heart of any good statistics site lies the statistics. To make it easier for you (since you don't have to save and upload a page from orac) and me (since I don't have to worry about people uploading incorrect/damaged pages), just punch in your orac username and password and your statistics will be fetched and updated automatically.

Please note that your orac password is not being stored, and is only used to request your orac cookie, and then use that cookie to retrieve a list of your problems from the hub; both the password and cookie are discarded immediately after the request is made. Your orac username will be stored in order to identify your statistics to others.
